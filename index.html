Since 1984, Zimmerman's Dry Goods has provided high-quality screen printing in the Twin Cities. We pride ourselves in an ability to bring you a product that exceeds all your expectations.

At Zimmerman's, we are proud to carry on the tradition of quality that was started in 1914 by Ben Zimmerman when he opened the first Zimmerman's Dry Goods in Minneapolis, selling everything from clothing to coffins. Zimmerman's has been a family business for three generations and we have always matched our expertise with a friendly and helpful staff.
